The Problem

Montgomery residents re-entering the workforce face interconnected barriers that generic job boards ignore. A single barrier — like no car or a criminal record — can disqualify someone from 60% of available jobs. Multiple barriers compound exponentially.

Fragmented Resources

Career centers, childcare providers, credit counselors, and legal aid exist — but there's no single system that connects them to a resident's specific situation.

Benefits Cliffs

Taking a job at the wrong wage can cost more in lost benefits than the paycheck provides. A $1/hr raise can mean losing $400/month in childcare subsidy.

Transit Blindness

Jobs that look great on paper are unreachable without a car. No Sunday bus service. Last bus at 9 PM kills second-shift jobs. Generic job boards don't know this.

Criminal Record Barriers

Alabama residents with records don't know which employers are fair-chance, whether they qualify for expungement, or how to navigate background check timing.

Key Features

Practical Value Score (PVS)

Jobs ranked by real-world value: 35% net income (with benefits cliff impact), 25% proximity, 20% schedule fit, 20% barrier compatibility. No-pay jobs penalized with 0.55x multiplier. Fair-chance employers boosted.

Scoring Engine

Benefits Cliff Detection

Calculates net income across $8-$25/hr in $0.50 steps for 7 Alabama programs. Flags severe cliffs (>$200 loss) so residents and case workers avoid wage traps. Visual cliff chart in the plan.

Financial Safety

Transit Schedule Matching

14 M-Transit routes, 42 stops. Validates shift times against first/last bus. Detects transfers via Rosa Parks hub. Flags no-Sunday and no-night service. Walk distance to nearest stop. Google Maps trip planning link.

Montgomery-Specific

Criminal Record Intelligence

Alabama Act 2021-507 expungement eligibility check. Fair-chance employer matching. Background check timing awareness (pre-offer vs. post-offer). Excluded charge category filtering.

Design Principles

✓

Privacy First: No accounts, no PII storage. Session-based with 30-day expiry. Credit data in browser sessionStorage only.

✓

Graceful Degradation: Every external service has a fallback. AI down? Template narrative. No BrightData? Seed jobs. No credit API? Skip credit scoring.

✓

City-Portable: Architecture is city-agnostic. Swap seed data (resources, transit, employers) and prompts for any city. Zero code changes.

✓

Feedback Loop: Resource health scoring via user feedback. Watch/Flagged/Hidden statuses. 30-day decay detection. QR-linked post-visit surveys.

✓

Security Hardened: Rate limiting, CORS, prompt injection defense, admin auth, input validation, Docker hardening, CI vulnerability scanning.
