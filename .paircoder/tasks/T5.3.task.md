# Objective

THE demo moment. "Here's what you can do Monday morning." Hero section that takes a ReEntryPlan and renders concrete, ordered action steps with times, locations, and specific instructions. Emotional core of the product.

# Implementation Plan

1. Accept ReEntryPlan + UserProfile as props
2. Hero header: "Here's what you can do Monday morning" in large Navy text
3. Numbered action step cards in vertical timeline layout:
   - Step number in Teal circle with connecting line
   - Action title (bold)
   - Location/address if available
   - Specific instruction ("Ask for HR", "Bring your ID and certification")
4. Steps derived from plan.immediate_next_steps + first matched resource + first job match
5. If AI narrative exists (plan.resident_summary), show as introductory paragraph in italic
6. Color: Navy headers, Teal step numbers, cards on off-white background
7. Bottom CTA: "Generate AI Summary" button (calls generateNarrative)
