# Objective

Remove the JSearch/RapidAPI integration entirely. BrightData multi-domain crawling (T31.1) replaces JSearch as the secondary job source. This eliminates the 200 req/month rate limit, removes a vendor dependency, and simplifies the job aggregation pipeline.
